About / Focus

While current research increasingly seeks to blur hierarchical boundaries through end-to-end learning and cross-layer optimization, hierarchical architectural thinking remains a fundamental foundation for robotics.

Hierarchical Robotic System: Decision-making → Predictive control → Reactive control, with safety across all layers.

My work focuses on integrating learning-based methods with optimization-based control for autonomous decision-making and robust physical interaction.
