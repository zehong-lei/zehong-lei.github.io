About Me

I am a researcher bridging the gap between Perception, Learning, and Control to enable robust robot autonomy in the physical world.

My work centers on Contact-Rich Interaction, addressing the core challenges of hybrid dynamics and constraints inherent in both legged locomotion and manipulation. Adopting a task-driven approach, I leverage both Reinforcement Learning for skill discovery in underactuated systems and Differentiable Physics for precise model-based reasoning. Ultimately, I focus on Sim-to-Real transfer, integrating accurate physical modeling and visuotactile perception to guarantee safety and high performance on real hardware.
