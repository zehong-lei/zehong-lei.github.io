Selected Projects

Dynamic manipulation of deformable linear objects (DLO)

Differentiable simulation and control prototyping for underactuated, contact-rich dynamics.

  • Built a GPU-accelerated simulation workflow to study elastic DLO dynamics and fast, whip-like motion primitives.
  • Designed an optimization-friendly pipeline for trajectory optimization / MPC-style rollouts and analysis.
  • Focused on practical modeling issues: stiffness/constraint tuning, contact handling, and failure modes.

Quadruped locomotion with deep reinforcement learning

Training, evaluation, and robustness testing in Isaac Gym / legged-gym style environments.

  • Set up a training pipeline for locomotion policies and standardized evaluation across terrains and disturbances.
  • Explored reward shaping, curriculum ideas, and domain randomization to improve robustness and generalization.
  • Built reproducible experiment scripts and logging to support fair comparisons across settings.
