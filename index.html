About Me

Hello! I am a robotics researcher with a background in Mechanical Engineering. I am currently completing my Master's degree at Politecnico di Milano, focusing on the intersection of classical Control Theory and modern AI.

My passion lies in Underactuated Systems. I am fascinated by the challenge of making robots not just move, but move intelligently and dynamically in the real world. I believe the most elegant robots should mimic the efficiency of biological counterparts rather than fighting against physics.

Currently, I am working on bridging the gap between simulation and reality (Sim-to-Real) using Differentiable Physics and Reinforcement Learning.
