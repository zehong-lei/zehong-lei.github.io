About Me

Hello! I am a robotics researcher with a background in Mechatronics and robotics. I am currently completing my Master's degree at Politecnico di Milano, focusing on the intersection of classical Control Theory and modern AI.

My passion lies in Underactuated Systems. I am fascinated by the challenge of making robots not just move, but move intelligently and dynamically in the real world. I believe the most elegant robots should mimic the efficiency of biological counterparts rather than fighting against physics.

Currently, I am working on bridging the gap between simulation and reality (Sim-to-Real) using Differentiable Physics and Deep Reinforcement Learning. In 2026, my goal is to grow into a Deep RL Algorithm Engineer for high-DoF robots, delivering end-to-end pipelines from simulation (Isaac Sim/Gym, MuJoCo, RaiSim, Gazebo, PyBullet, CoppeliaSim) to ROS 2-based real-robot validation.
